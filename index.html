How to choose the right system

A good sales conversation should not start with model numbers. It should start with production needs, air quality and energy cost.

Customer needRecommended directionWhy it matters
General factory air supply✓ Oil-injected compressor + dryerBalanced cost and performance for broad industrial usage.
Cleaner air requirement✓ Oil-free compressor + treatment systemReduces oil contamination risk in sensitive production processes.
High humidity / moisture risk✓ Refrigerated or adsorption dryerProtects equipment and improves air quality stability.
Large utility / heavy industry✓ Engineered air station configurationSupports continuous operation and easier maintenance planning.
